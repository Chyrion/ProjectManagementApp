Project Management App

This is a project management application, in which users can create personal or collaborative projects. Projects are made up of tasks, which can be assigned to users who are part of the project.

Projects keep track of their status. Fresh projects will be marked as 'not started', but when tasks are added (but not completed), the project moves to 'in progress'. Once all tasks are marked as complete, the project may also be marked complete!

Projects and tasks also have deadlines! Each one will show how long it is until the given deadline. Once the deadline has passed, the project or task will be marked as 'Late!'. However, this does not prevent completion, and projects/tasks may be marked as completed even after the deadline has passed.
